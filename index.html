Frequently Asked Questions

How does NyaySaathi protect my privacy?

NyaySaathi uses end-to-end encryption to protect all your communications and data. We follow a zero-knowledge architecture where our servers cannot access your unencrypted information. All data is stored securely and you have full control over your privacy settings.

Is the AI advice legally binding?

No, the AI-generated content is for educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. It's designed to help you understand your situation better and prepare for consultation with qualified lawyers. Always consult a licensed attorney for binding legal advice.

How are lawyers verified on the platform?

All lawyers on NyaySaathi are verified through a comprehensive process including Bar Council registration verification, credential checks, and background verification. We ensure only qualified and ethical legal professionals are available on our platform.

What happens to my case data?

Your case data is securely stored and only shared with lawyers you explicitly choose to consult. You can configure data retention periods in your settings, and you have the right to delete your data at any time. We comply with all applicable data protection regulations.
